Exploring Eczema

Eczema, is chronic skin condition that causes inflamed, itchy, and red patches of skin. It can affect individuals of any age but is most common in children. The main symptoms include severe itching, especially at night, and red or brownish-gray patches found on the hands, eyelids, and the inside bend of elbows and knees.
